About

My name is Dainan Caywood. I am a storm chaser, photographer, and weather systems developer focused on documenting severe weather across the United States.

My interests include supercell structure, tornado dynamics, radar visualization, and atmospheric field observation.

In the field, I combine forecasting analysis with high-resolution photography to capture both the science and intensity of severe weather events.

  • Wide and telephoto lenses
  • Mobile radar and forecasting tools
