About Me

My name is Dainan Caywood, and I am a dedicated storm chaser and photographer with a passion for documenting severe weather. From supercells to tornado outbreaks, I pursue storms to capture their structure, intensity, and impact.

Beyond photography, I develop weather-based software systems, radar visualization tools, and real-time alert platforms. My work combines field experience with technical innovation.
