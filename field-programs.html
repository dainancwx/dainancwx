Field Programs

STRATUS field programs are at the core of our mission deploying advanced weather instrumentation and research teams to collect real-world data on storm systems, boundary layers, and atmospheric structure.

  • Conduct hands-on field research across diverse regions of the United States, targeting a wide range of severe weather environments
  • Maintain dedicated technical and administrative support to enable complex, multi-platform deployments
  • Deploy advanced sensor networks, mobile mesonets, and experimental probes for real-time data collection and analysis
  • Collaborate with universities, government agencies, and research teams to support integrated and cooperative field operations
  • Prioritize safe, efficient, and high-impact missions that advance storm-scale atmospheric science
