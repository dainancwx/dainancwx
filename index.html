Welcome

I am a U.S.-based storm chaser and photographer focused on capturing supercells, tornadoes, and extreme weather events across the country. My work combines field documentation, forecasting analysis, and technical development.

In addition to photography, I build weather dashboards, radar visualization systems, and automated alert tools designed for real-time storm tracking.
